Search Engine Optimisatio (SEO)

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the practice of optimizing websites and online content to rank higher on search engine results pages (SERPs), primarily on Google. The goal is to increase organic (non-paid) traffic to a website.

Social Media Marketing (SMM)

Social Media Marketing (SMM) is the process of using social media platforms to promote products, services, or brands. It involves strategies to create and share content that engages users, builds brand awareness, and drives conversions.

Web Marketing

Web marketing, also known as digital marketing or online marketing, refers to promoting products or services using the internet. It includes a wide range of strategies and channels to reach and engage with potential customers. Since you're a freelance digital marketer specializing in SEO, SMM, and SEM, you might already be familiar with some of the key aspects.

Serach Engine Marketing(SEM)

Search Engine Marketing (SEM) is a digital marketing strategy that focuses on increasing a website’s visibility in search engine results pages (SERPs) through paid advertising. It primarily involves pay-per-click (PPC) advertising, where businesses bid on keywords to have their ads displayed when users search for related terms.

Content Marketing

Content marketing is a digital marketing strategy focused on creating, publishing, and distributing valuable content to attract and engage a target audience. Unlike traditional advertising, content marketing builds trust, authority, and brand awareness by offering helpful and relevant information. .

Email Marketing

Email marketing is a powerful digital marketing strategy that involves sending emails to prospects and customers to promote products, nurture relationships, and drive conversions. When done correctly, it can generate high ROI (Return on Investment) and build long-term customer loyalty.
